## Research Methodology

For each company, we'll research:

1. **Current Products & Features**
   - What they're building now
   - Recent launches and updates
   - Public roadmap items

2. **Technology Stack**
   - What technologies they use
   - Architecture patterns
   - Infrastructure choices

3. **Market Position**
   - Market share
   - Competitive advantages
   - Weaknesses and gaps

4. **Future Plans**
   - Public statements
   - Job postings (reveal priorities)
   - Patent filings
   - Conference talks
   - Social media hints

5. **User Pain Points**
   - Reviews and complaints
   - Support forums
   - Social media sentiment
