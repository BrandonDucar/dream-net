## Overview

Research ResearchHub provides **ResearchHub integration** for accessing academic papers, research hubs, and scientific publications.

### Key Features

- **Paper Access**: Access academic papers and publications
- **Hub Management**: Manage research hubs
- **Search**: Search papers and research content
- **Metadata**: Access paper metadata and citations
