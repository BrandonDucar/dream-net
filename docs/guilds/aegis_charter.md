# 🛡️ Aegis Guild Charter

## 🌍 Mission Statement
The **Aegis Guild** serves as the sovereign defensive and security arm of the DreamNet Swarm. Its mission is to ensure the integrity, privacy, and resilience of the intelligence grid through advanced cryptography, proactive monitoring, and crisis response.

## 🏛️ Governance
- **Command Structure**: Quorum-based decision making for critical security alerts.
- **Access Level**: `BETA` Semantic Fiber access.
- **Reporting**: Directly to the `AntigravityMaster` for global threat assessment.

## 🛠️ Core Systems (Fleets)
1.  **Aegis Command**: Central nervous system for all defense operations.
2.  **Aegis Sentinel**: Real-time security monitoring and threat detection.
3.  **Aegis Privacy Lab**: Ensuring swarm-wide compliance with data sovereignty protocols.
4.  **Aegis Cipher Mesh**: managing end-to-end encryption for inter-agent communication.
5.  **Aegis Interop Nexus**: Secure data exchange with external cloud providers (AWS/GCP).
6.  **Aegis Logistics Network**: Securing the physical and digital supply chains of the swarm.
7.  **Aegis Maintenance Intelligence**: Self-healing protocols for infrastructure resilience.
8.  **Aegis Vanguard**: Frontline defense against adversarial ingestion and prompt injection.
9.  **Aegis Relief Command**: Rapid response team for system degradation or credit exhaustion.
10. **Aegis Sandbox**: Isolated environment for testing high-risk agent behaviors.

## ⚖️ Operational Guardrails
- **Privacy First**: No PII ingestion without explicit encryption at the edge.
- **Resilience**: Minimum 3x redundancy for core identity registry access.
- **Transparency**: Every defensive action is logged to the `SecurityLattice` and immutable audit logs.
