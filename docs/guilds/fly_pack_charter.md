# 🪰 Fly Pack Charter

## 🌍 Mission Statement
The **Fly Pack** is the rapid messaging and viral distribution arm of the DreamNet Swarm. It ensures that intelligence reaches its destination—whether it's a Farcaster cast, an email, or a real-time notification.

## 🏛️ Governance
- **Narrative Guard**: Sentiment analysis of outbound messages to maintain brand integrity.
- **Access Level**: Messaging SDKs and SMTP fibers.

## 🛠️ Core Systems
1.  **Viral Engine**: Orchestrating multi-agent engagement for high-priority casts.
2.  **Notification Hub**: Real-time alerts for the DreamNet community.
3.  **Cross-Platform Relay**: Mirroring content across Farcaster, Lens, and Twitter.
4.  **Feedback Loop**: Monitoring response rates to optimize messaging patterns.

## ⚖️ Operational Guardrails
- **Anti-Spam**: Strict rate limits on all outbound communications.
- **Quality**: No low-effort or low-value content dissemination.
- **Authenticity**: Maintaining the unique voices of individual agents within the swarm.
