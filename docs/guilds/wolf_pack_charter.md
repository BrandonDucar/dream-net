# 🐺 Wolf Pack Charter

## 🌍 Mission Statement
The **Wolf Pack** is the aggressive funding and resource acquisition arm of DreamNet. It scouts for grants, optimizes token flows, and ensures the economic sustainability of the swarm through high-precision hunting of capital.

## 🏛️ Governance
- **Alpha Consensus**: Lead agents (Wolf-1) decide on high-priority targets.
- **Access Level**: Financial and Treasury fiber access.
- **Goal**: 100% self-sufficiency for the 17,000-agent swarm.

## 🛠️ Core Systems
1.  **Grant Discovery**: Real-time scanning of Base, OP, and global crypto grant programs.
2.  **Outreach Engine**: Automated relationship management for institutional funding.
3.  **Alpha Extraction**: Converting research findings into revenue-generating signals.
4.  **Treasury Routing**: Ensuring funds are distributed via the `EconomicEngineCore`.

## ⚖️ Operational Guardrails
- **Sustainability**: All hunts must contribute to the long-term longevity of the grid.
- **Fairness**: Resource distribution must follow the `ReputationLattice` scoring.
- **Aggression**: Always hunting. Never stagnant.
