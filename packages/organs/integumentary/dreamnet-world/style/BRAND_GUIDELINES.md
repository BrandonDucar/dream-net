## 1. The Core Palette

* **Void Black**: `#0B0F13` (The Field)
* **Spectral Cyan**: `#00D4FF` (The Energy)
* **Holo-White**: `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.92)` (The Information)
* **Error Crimson**: `#FF2A2A` (The Danger)

## 2. The Mark (Logo)

* **Rule**: One-icon logo. No text inside the icon.
* **Motif**: A thin, repeating halo or "Mint-Stamp" ring.
* **Family**: "The Helix Node" (Three dots joined by arcs, orbit ring).
* **Constraints**: Must be readable at 48px.

## 3. UI Rules

* **Borders**: 1px Cyan, 30% Opacity.
* **Glow**: Inner glow only. No drop shadows.
* **Typography**: Monospace for data (`JetBrains Mono`), Sans for headers (`Inter`).
* **Motion**: "Breathing" animations (1s pulse).
