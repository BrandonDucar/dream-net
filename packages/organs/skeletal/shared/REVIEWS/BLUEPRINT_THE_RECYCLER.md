## 3. The Revenue Streams

### Stream A: "The Blacklist" (Selling Defense)

* **The Waste**: IP addresses and wallet addresses that tried to attack/spam us.
* **The Product**: A real-time "Threat Intelligence Feed."
* **Buyer**: Security companies, other DAOs.
* **Value**: "Don't let these guys in."

### Stream B: "The Failure Dataset" (Selling Training Data)

* **The Waste**: 1,000 failed grant proposals.
* **The Product**: A high-value RLHF dataset for training AI *what not to do*.
* **Buyer**: AI Labs (OpenAI/Anthropic need negative examples).
* **Value**: "Here is a dataset of hallucinations and failures."

### Stream C: "The Dead Link Empire" (SEO Squatting)

* **The Waste**: External URLs that our crawlers found to be dead (404).
* **The Product**: We buy the expired domain immediately.
* **Buyer**: Backlink SEO agencies.
* **Value**: "This domain has 10,000 backlinks but the owner forgot to renew. It is ours now."
