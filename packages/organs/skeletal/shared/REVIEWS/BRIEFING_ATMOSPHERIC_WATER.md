## 1. Intelligence Synthesis: Water from Thin Air

In 2026, water scarcity is a strategic vulnerability. **Atmospheric Water Generation (AWG)** allows for the creation of decentralized, infrastructure-free water supplies by harvesting moisture from the air.

### Key Technical Concepts

- **Hygroscopic Desiccants**: Materials (like those used by Drupps) that absorb moisture even at low humidity, then release it when heated.
- **Solar Hydropanels**: (SOURCE Global) Fully autonomous panels that use solar thermal energy to drive the evaporation-condensation cycle, requiring no external electricity.
- **Metal-Organic Frameworks (MOFs)**: Advanced porous materials that can trap water molecules with extreme efficiency, potentially miniaturizing AWG for individual agents (or drones).
