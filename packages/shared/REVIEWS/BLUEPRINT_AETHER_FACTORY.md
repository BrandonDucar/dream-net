**Objective**: To decouple DreamNet's physical supply chain from "Ground Sources" (Mines/Wells).

## 1. The Hijack: "The Sabatier Loop"

We use DAC to get CO2. We use Electrolysis to get H2. We combine them (Sabatier/Fischer-Tropsch) to print Methane (Rocket Fuel) or Ethylene (Plastic Feedstock).

## 2. Architecture

- **Input**: Air + Sunlight + Water.
- **Process**: `CO2 + 4H2 -> CH4 + 2H2O`.
- **Output**: Liquefied Methane (LNG) or Polyethylene blocks for 3D printers.

## 3. Implementation Logic

1. **Deploy**: A containerized DAC unit (e.g., from Heimdal or localized tech).
2. **Power**: Excess solar (the "duck curve" waste).
3. **Stockpile**: Instead of batteries, we store energy as *Liquid Methane*. It has infinite shelf life and high density.
