## 1. Intelligence Synthesis: The Star in the Sky

Terrestrial solar is limited by night and weather. **Space-Based Solar Power (SBSP)** harvests energy in GEO where the sun never sets, then beams it down via microwaves. 2026 saw Caltech's SSPP successfully beam power to Earth.

### Key Technical Concepts

- **Microwave Phased Arrays**: Thousands of tiny antennae on a satellite firing in perfect unison to steer a power beam without moving parts (MAPLE).
- **Rectennas**: "Rectifying Antennas" on the ground that convert incoming 2.45 GHz microwaves back into DC electricity with high efficiency.
- **Modular Architecture**: Instead of one giant Death Star, using swarms of flat-packed, self-assembling tiles (see QL-24) to reduce launch costs.
