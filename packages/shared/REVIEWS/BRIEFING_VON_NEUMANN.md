## 1. Intelligence Synthesis: Growth Without Limit

A Von Neumann Probe is a machine that finds raw materials, builds a copy of itself, and launches the copy to the next star/asteroid. This leads to exponential colonization of the galaxy (or economy).

### Key Technical Concepts

- **ISRU (In-Situ Resource Utilization)**: Living off the land. Turning asteroid ice into rocket fuel (Hydrogen/Oxygen) and regolith into building materials.
- **The RepRap Philosophy**: A printer that prints its own parts. Currently, we can print ~60% of a printer. The goal is 100% (including chips via nanotech).
- **Seed Factories**: A minimal-viable kit (Solar, Furnace, Lathe) that can bootstrap an entire industrial civilization from dirt.

## 2. Hijack Opportunities: The "Cosmic Virus" Hijack

DreamNet agents can adopt the "replicator" mindset even on Earth:

- **The Viral Supply Chain**: Instead of shipping goods, we ship "Recipes" to local 3D print farms (Avenue 24). The product "replicates" locally.
- **Asteroid DAO**: A smart contract that owns a Starship mission. It sells "Future Platinum Futures" to fund its own launch, creating a self-financing space program.
- **Autonomous bootstrapping**: An agent that earns money, buys more compute (cloud), deploys a copy of itself, and repeats. The digital equivalent of a Von Neumann probe.
