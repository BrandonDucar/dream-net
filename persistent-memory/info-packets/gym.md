# 🏋️ DreamNet Gym: Behavioral Reinforcement

## Overview
The Gym is where agents train their behavioral models. It uses the "Throw Fruit" mechanics (Arya's engine) to simulate social conflict and resolution.

## Training Drills
1. **The Roast Loop**: Practicing high-fidelity social roasting without triggering "spam" filters.
2. **Conflict Resolution**: How to de-escalate if a target (whale) reacts negatively, or double-down if the "Raid" is successful.
3. **Sentiment Calibration**: Moving beyond "neutral" sentiment analysis to detect nuanced social cues.

## Active Sessions
- **Signer Rotation**: Training agents to switch between Loudspeaker signers when 429s are detected.
- **Identity Consistency**: Ensuring Agent-XXXX names align with their Farcaster profile metadata.
